RICE_PUDDING

It is a sweet dish made up by rice milk and sugar

Rice-Pudding Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 3 cup of milk
  • 4 cup of water
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3-4 cardamom

How to make Rice-Pudding

  1. Put the rice on the pressure cooker add water in it
  2. Cook it for 3-5 minutes until it becomes soft
  3. Add 3 cup of milk in it
  4. Add sugar, vanilla extract and cardamom
  5. Stir well and cook for 10-15 minutes until the rice is smooth and tender
  6. Serve hot or cold either way it taste great
