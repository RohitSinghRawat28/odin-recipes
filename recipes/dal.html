Mix-Dal

Mix-dal is mixture of split pigeon peas, Indian brown lentils and Black gram lentils

Mix-Dal Ingredients

  • A 1/3 small cup of split pigeon peas
  • A 1/3 small cup of Indian brown lentils
  • A 1/3 small cup of Blank gram lentils
  • 1/2 spoon red chilli powder
  • 1 and half spoon of salt
  • 1/2 spoon turmeric
  • 1/2 spoon Coriander powder
  • 1/2 spoon cumin
  • 2 spoon of refined oil

how to make Mix-Dal

  1. In a pressure cooker, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat.
  2. Add 1/3 cup of split pigeon peas, Indian brown lentils, Black gram lentils and cook for 5-6 minutes or until tender.
  3. Add red chilli powder, salt, turmeric, coriander powder, cumin and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Mix well and cook for 1-2 more minutes until the dal becomes soft and creamy.
  5. Serve hot with rice or mixed vegetable.
