TEA

Tea is a famous hot drink of India it is refreshing and can also be called red bull of India

Tea ingredients

  • tea leaves
  • sugar
  • water
  • milk
  • cardamom

How to make tea

  1. Put medium sized container in stove
  2. Add 1 cup of water in it
  3. Add 2 tablespoon sugar
  4. Add half spoon chai-patti
  5. Add 1 or 2 cardamom
  6. Let it boil for 2-3 minutes in medium flame
  7. Add 1 cup of milk
  8. Let it boil for more 5 minutes
  9. Turn off the stove and your tea is ready to serve
